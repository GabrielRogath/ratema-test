RATEMA

RAFIKI TEHAMA KWA MAENDELEO YA JAMII

RATEMA is a Non Governmental Organization registered under Tanzanian laws Act No.24 of 2002 with registration No: 0906. RATEMA is an acronym of Kiswahili terms "Rakifi Tehama kwa Maendeleo ya Jamii" which means a Friendly Technologies for Community Development.

RATEMA has realizing that there's a gap of insufficiently utilizing or using of technologies to make our communities more sustainable places to live, work and socialize in. RATEMA is focusing on eliminate or reduce this gap by creating open and innovative projects that can enrich communities and provide social support to the socities. With these projects created will also increase the level of understanding and awareness in the aspects of Health and Wellbeing, Social Development, Environmental and Educational

Projects

SDGsPamoja App

SDGsPamoja App is Kiswahili and English mobile application developed to promote the UN's Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and encourage local communities to engage directly with actions that lead to the achievement of the goals by 2030.

SDGsPamoja App has a platform that allows users to show their actions that will lead or mobilize the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals in our communities.

Also SDGsPamoja app has a platform that allows users to engage in chats so as to be able to share their ideas and ways that can be used to solve or tackle different problems in our societies in order to achieve of the SDGs in our communities.

Also there are two distinguished platforms which removes the language barrier, These platforms explains all the 17 UN's SDGs in both English and Kiswahili so as they can give a clear understanding of the UN's Sustainable Development Goals to the local communities.
