SDGsPamoja App

Project description, August 3, 2019

A Kiswahili and English mobile application developed to promote the UN’s Sustainable Development Goals (SDG’s) and encourage local communities to engage directly with actions that lead to the achievement of the goals by 2030.

The App is now available for Android devices,Just type SDGsPamoja in Google Play Store without a space to install it in your Android phone, or Click Here to view it on Google play store.

SDGsPamoja App has a platform that allows users to show their actions that will lead or mobilize the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals in our communities.

Also SDGsPamoja app has a platform that allows users to engage in chats so as to be able to share their ideas and ways that can be used to solve or tackle different problems in our societies in order to achieve of the SDGs in our communities.

Also there are two distinguished platforms which removes the language barrier, These platforms explains all the 17 UN's SDGs in both English and Kiswahili so as they can give a clear understanding of the UN's Sustainable Development Goals to the local communities.
